Welcome to Keeping Tabs

Keeping Tabs is a Chrome Extension that looks to make your life easier by keeping track of which tabs to close. If you've ever had an issue with having too many tabs opened then this is the extension for you! Keeping Tabs will close tabs based on specific settings you control. Want to close the tabs you haven't visited in a while? There's a setting for that! Want to keep tabs that relate to certain topics? There's a setting for that! Download the extension in the Chrome Store today!
